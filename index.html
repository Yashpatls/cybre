What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like cell phones, computers, and tablets.

Harassment

Sending offensive, rude, and insulting messages to someone repeatedly.

  • Repeated hurtful messages
  • Name-calling and insults
  • Threatening language

Impersonation

Pretending to be someone else and sending or posting material to damage that person's reputation.

  • Creating fake profiles
  • Posting as someone else
  • Sharing false information

Exclusion

Intentionally leaving someone out of an online group, conversation, or activity.

  • Being left out of group chats
  • Purposely excluding from online games
  • Ignoring someone's messages
